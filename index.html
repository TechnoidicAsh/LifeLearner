Our Goals

Vision, Mission & Purpose Behind Life Learner

Educate

To educate everyone about the free knowledge available online in whatsoever they wish to learn.

Leverage

Learn to get the maximum out of what you have, where you are.

Independence

Everyone is unique and has his/her learning abilities. You should be allowed to identify your "way", the way you want it.

Ownership Mindset

Be responsible and stand at your commitments. Learn that bad is for the betterment of you.

Change For Good

It is the only constant. Learn to unlearn things, especially the indoctrinations taught by society.

Character Before Competence

Remember your ideals, values and morale before practising your skills.
