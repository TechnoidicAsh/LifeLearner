About LifeLearner

The main motive behind this project was to bring awareness among people, about the amount of information that is available online for FREE. It felt like the "Gold Mine" was open for all, yet everyone took it as a "Coal Mine". In a nutshell, all I wanted to do was:

  • Pass on the baton
  • Build Something that's "TRUELY FREE" (users aren't the product)
  • Help anyone in the way I can (with "Art Of Googling")
